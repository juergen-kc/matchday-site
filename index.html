104 matches, 39 days, four-plus time zones — turned into a short, personal plan of the matches you can actually watch, in your local time. The official app is the firehose; Matchday is the filter. Native, offline, on-device.

Highlights

  • Watchability tags: watchable / during work / late / overnight
  • Favorite teams always in your plan, flagged when they fall at odd hours
  • Home Screen widget — your next match with a live countdown
  • A terminal knockout bracket that fills in as the groups finish
  • Two-tap local reminders & one-tap calendar holds — no account, no server
  • Four palettes: paper, green phosphor, amber CRT, mono
  • Works offline; refreshes itself when online — a monospace “terminal” design
