FAQ

The times look off — are they wrong?

Every kickoff renders in your device's current time zone. If you travel, reopen the app and times re-render automatically. The detail screen also shows the venue's local time.

My reminder didn't fire.

Reminders are local notifications. Make sure notifications are enabled for Matchday in Settings › Notifications, and that you set the reminder before its lead time passed.

I added a match to my calendar but want to remove it.

Matchday uses write-only calendar access and can't read your calendar back, so remove the event in the Calendar app. You can re-add it from the match's detail screen.

Where are live scores and the bracket?

This release is a forward-looking watch-planner. Scores, standings, and the knockout bracket are planned for a later update.

Is this the official app?

No. Matchday is independent and unofficial, not affiliated with or endorsed by any governing body, tournament organizer, broadcaster, or team. Match data is factual and times are subject to change.
