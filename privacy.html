Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Matchday collects no personal data and makes no network requests. It is a fully on-device app with no backend.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

What stays on your device

Your preferences — favorite teams, watch window, reminder lead times, and which matches you've set a reminder or calendar hold for — are stored only on your device. They never leave it.

Notifications & Calendar

Reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device; nothing is sent to a server. Calendar holds use the system's write-only calendar permission: the app can add an event you choose, but cannot read your calendar. Both permissions are optional and requested only when you tap the corresponding action.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
